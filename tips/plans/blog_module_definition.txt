3. Blog module: +2,500 EGP
** What is blog module?

`Blog module` means adding a full Articles/News section to the website.

For the `+2,500 EGP` add-on, it usually includes:
1. Blog listing page (all articles).
2. Single article page design.
3. Admin ability to add/edit/delete posts.
4. Categories (for example: Weight Loss, PCOS, Diet Plans).
5. Basic SEO fields per article (title, meta description, URL slug).

Usually not included in that price:
1. Writing article content.
2. Ongoing monthly publishing.
3. Manual translation of every post (unless agreed).

For a nutrition doctor, it is useful for SEO and trust, but if budget is tight, you can launch without it and add it later.
