## Why not 120,000 EGP right now?

That number is defensible **only if** you have:
- A portfolio of 3–5 similar sites to show
- A team or at least a designer working with you
- Established client reviews/referrals

Without those, if the client asks "show me similar work you've done," you'll lose the deal or feel pressured to discount heavily. It's better to win at 65,000 EGP, deliver exceptionally, and charge 120,000+ on your next clinic project with this one as a case study.
