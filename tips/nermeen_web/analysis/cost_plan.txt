
Cost Model (USD)
========================

One-Time Build Cost Ranges
1) Lean MVP (single language, booking + contact, no e-commerce complexity)
- Design + setup + launch: 1,200 to 2,500

2) Professional Clinic (closest to benchmark: multilingual + booking + WooCommerce)
- Design + setup + content migration + launch: 3,000 to 7,000

3) Premium Growth Build (custom UX, advanced automations, stronger SEO foundation)
- Full implementation: 8,000 to 15,000+


Recurring Costs (Monthly / Annual)
- Hosting (managed WordPress/LiteSpeed): 20-100 per month
- Cloudflare: 0 (free) to 20 per month (Pro)
- Domain: 10-20 per year
- WPML: ~99 per year (if selected)
- Premium builder/theme licenses: 60-120 per year
- Security/backup premium plugins: 0-200 per year
- Maintenance retainer (updates, backups, fixes): 150-600 per month




Expected Baseline Annual Running Cost
===================================
- Lean operations: ~500-1,500 per year (without maintenance retainer)
- Professional operations: ~1,500-4,000 per year (including paid tools and better hosting)

Team Plan
- 1 WordPress developer
- 1 UI/UX designer (part-time during first 2-3 weeks)
- 1 QA/content support role (part-time before launch)

Budget Decision Guide
============================
- If you need fast launch and low cost: choose Lean MVP.
- If you need close resemblance + bilingual + booking + sales: choose Professional Clinic.######################
- If growth and conversion optimization are strategic: choose Premium Growth Build.

Procurement Checklist Before Starting
- Domain and hosting account ownership.
- Brand assets and approved content (Arabic/English if bilingual).
- Payment gateway merchant account.
- WhatsApp business number and email SMTP provider.
- Legal pages (privacy policy, cookies, terms, consent text).

Cost Calculation Formula
- Total Year-1 Cost = One-time Build + (Monthly Recurring x 12) + Annual Licenses

Example (Professional Clinic Midpoint)
- Build: 5,000
- Recurring: 180/month x 12 = 2,160
- Annual licenses/tools: 300
- Estimated Year-1 Total: 7,460
