## The Important Insight

Your **actual out-of-pocket cost as the developer is ~27,000 EGP** in licenses — and that's if you buy everything new.

### You have two options:

**Option A — Bundle everything**
You buy all licenses, include them in your quote. Client pays one number. Simpler for them.
→ Your 65,000 EGP quote already covers this. Your pure profit is ~38,000 EGP.

**Option B — Separate licenses (recommended)**
Client buys hosting + domain + WPML + Amelia directly under their own account. You only buy what's a developer tool (Elementor Pro, theme).
→ Your real cost drops to ~6,000–7,000 EGP. Profit on a 65,000 EGP quote becomes ~58,000 EGP.

**Option B is better** because:
- Client owns their own licenses (no dependency on you)
- Your risk is lower
- Easier to hand off later
- More professional and transparent
