### The key variable: are you a freelancer or a small studio?

Freelancers in Egypt typically charge **30–50% less than agencies** (roughly 200–500 EGP/hour), while agencies run 400–1,000 EGP/hour and provide dedicated teams, project management, and QA.

This means:

| Who you are | Realistic range for this scope |
|---|---|
| Solo freelancer | 50,000 – 90,000 EGP |
| Small studio / 2–3 person team | 90,000 – 140,000 EGP |
| Established agency | 120,000 – 250,000 EGP |

If you're presenting yourself as a **solo developer**, 120,000 EGP may face pushback — not because it's wrong, but because Egyptian clients mentally anchor freelancers to lower rates. If you position yourself as a **small professional studio**, the same number is completely normal.
